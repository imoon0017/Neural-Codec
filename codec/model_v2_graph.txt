DESIGN NOTES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  DownBlock  — decouples feature extraction (stride-1 Conv2d, full
               cross-channel mixing) from spatial downsampling
               (depthwise stride-2 Conv2d, per-channel spatial filter).
               The depthwise conv has a single spatial job so training
               pressure naturally drives it toward low-pass behaviour,
               avoiding aliasing at the decimation step.

  UpBlock    — 4×4 ConvTranspose2d (stride=2, padding=1) gives an exact
               ×2 upsample with a learnable filter. Replaces PixelShuffle
               which rearranges a fixed spatial block into channels and
               produces chessboard artefacts when the mapping is not
               perfectly learned.
